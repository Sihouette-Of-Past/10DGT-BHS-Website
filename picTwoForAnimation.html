Fungi Whale

This is a whale I made with special mushrooms on its back. It was inspired by a digital art of mushrooms on ants.

For this image, I used Google to find images of whales and mushrooms for ideas. Then I used Inkscape to make the whale, the seaweed plants, and the mushrooms with beizer lines. Then I choose colours and gradients for them to look aesthetic. Finally, I bolded the lines and added final touches to create this picture.
