Lydia's Colourful Background

This is my first year 10 Digi Tech project. It was inspired by a cloud I saw looking outside instead of focusing.

For this image, I found some interesting objects on the internet, and used Gimp to seperate them from their background. I then arranged them into nice spots, and added a semi-transparent blue background. Finally, I found an image of an open book to be the background.
